Working in Sylvania

Sylvania's housing mix runs from mid-century builds (1950s-70s) in the older neighborhoods near Main Street and Tam-O-Shanter through newer Sylvania Township subdivisions from the 2000s and 2010s. Most of what we see is standard forced-air: gas furnaces, central AC, a mix of tank water heaters, with mini-splits showing up more often for additions and converted basements.

The mid-century homes are usually on their second or third HVAC system by now. Ductwork is generally in decent shape. Furnace and AC replacements in this area are typically straightforward like-for-like swaps, sometimes with a sizing recalculation if previous contractors guessed wrong.

Newer Sylvania Township builds are usually still on their original equipment. We see tune-up calls on those more than repairs.
