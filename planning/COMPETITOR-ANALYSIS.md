# Toledo HVAC Competitor Analysis

Top 5 competitors for "HVAC Toledo OH" / "AC repair Toledo" / "furnace repair Toledo."

## 1. Fry Heating, Cooling & Plumbing — fryheating.com
- **Size/age:** ~90 years, multi-office (Toledo + Ann Arbor), HVAC + plumbing + electrical
- **Pages:** 80+ pages. Per-service pages, 18 city pages (10 OH incl. Perrysburg, Sylvania, Waterville, Whitehouse / 8 MI), industry verticals (multifamily, hospitality, healthcare)
- **Content:** Blog, dedicated FAQ hub (HVAC/plumbing/sewer), financing, federal tax credits, VIP membership
- **Trust:** 1,000+ Google reviews, Trane/Rheem/York partner, veteran-owned, BBB profile, 24/7
- **Schema:** LocalBusiness + likely Service schema (large site, robust SEO infrastructure)

## 5 Content Gaps Maverick & Miller Should Fill

1. **City landing pages for all 12 NW Ohio cities** — only Fry covers Perrysburg/Sylvania/Waterville/Whitehouse. Nobody covers Port Clinton, Elmore, Woodville, Oregon, Northwood, Point Place. Huge local SEO opening.
2. **FAQ hub with schema** — only Fry has one. Add FAQPage JSON-LD for AC/furnace/water heater/mini-split questions to win featured snippets.
3. **Seasonal + problem-specific blog** ("furnace won't ignite," "AC frozen coil," "heat pump vs. furnace NW Ohio winters") — M&M has a blog but topics are thin; others have none.
4. **LocalBusiness + HVACBusiness + Service schema** — competitors are weak here. Proper JSON-LD with service area, hours, geo coordinates, aggregateRating is a quick win.
5. **Transparent trust stack on homepage** — BBB A+ badge, founding year, license number, financing partner logo, Google review count with live star widget. Most competitors bury these; M&M founded-2024 story + A+ BBB is a differentiator if presented prominently alongside founder bios.

**Bonus differentiators:** mini-split / ductless dedicated page (most ignore it), commercial HVAC sub-site (only Fry/M&M have real commercial depth), financing calculator page, same-day service guarantee with time window.
