# Backlink Strategy — Maverick & Miller HVAC

Off-site plan. Documented for Janon to execute after site ships. Not a build task.

## Quick wins (do first, high ROI)

1. **BBB listing** — Maverick is already A+ BBB. Ensure their BBB profile links to maverickandmillerhvac.com (dofollow, ~DA 92)
2. **Chamber of Commerce memberships** — Toledo Regional (~$500/yr), Perrysburg Area (~$300/yr), Sylvania Area (~$300/yr). Members get dofollow directory links. DA 40-60 each.
3. **Google Business Profile** — technically not a backlink but seeds everything else
4. **Yelp, Angi, Houzz, Nextdoor, Facebook Business** — free directory citations (covered in LOCAL-SEO.md)
5. **Supplier/brand partner links** — if Maverick is a certified dealer for Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, they get a "dealer locator" link. Worth asking. DA 70+.

## Medium effort (3-6 month timeline)

6. **Local news / PR**
   - Toledo Blade sponsorship or small-business feature
   - Sentinel-Tribune (Bowling Green) — similar angle for BG-area coverage
   - Family business angle (founded 2024, two founders, first year story)
7. **HVAC industry directories**
   - ACCA.org (if Maverick is a member)
   - ASHRAE local chapter
   - Service Roundtable (paid membership, member directory)
8. **Local nonprofit sponsorships** — youth sports team jerseys, community events, school fundraisers in Perrysburg / Sylvania / Bowling Green all link back
9. **Guest posts on related NW Ohio blogs** — home improvement, real estate, moving guides
10. **Local real estate agents** — partner with agents for post-purchase HVAC inspections; they link from "trusted vendors" page on their site

## Higher effort (ongoing, 6-12 months)

11. **HARO / Qwoted / Source Bottle** — respond to journalist queries on HVAC topics. Takes time but yields DA 80+ publications occasionally
12. **Content-driven links** — publish genuinely useful local content that earns links:
    - "Toledo home heating bill calculator"
    - "Is my furnace too old? Age-by-brand guide for NW Ohio homes"
    - "Mini-split vs central air for Perrysburg historic homes"
    - Tools and calculators earn more links than blog posts
13. **Scholarship programs** — $500 HVAC trade scholarship for Penta Career Center or Owens Community College students. Education domain links (.edu DA 70-90)

## What to avoid

- Link farms, PBNs, paid link networks
- Guest post mills (sites that publish anyone for $50)
- "1000 backlinks for $5" services — actively harmful
- Exact-match anchor text on external links (over-optimization penalty)
- Reciprocal link exchanges

## Anchor text strategy (for inbound links)

Natural distribution target:
- 40% branded ("Maverick & Miller HVAC", "maverickandmillerhvac.com")
- 25% naked URL ("https://maverickandmillerhvac.com")
- 20% generic ("click here", "learn more", "visit site")
- 10% partial match ("Toledo HVAC company", "HVAC contractor")
- 5% exact match ("hvac toledo", "ac repair toledo")

Don't request specific anchors when asking for links — let it happen naturally. Over-optimization looks manipulative.
