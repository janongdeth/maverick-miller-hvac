How we price

We post ranges on every service page. Not because we can quote your specific job without seeing it, but because we believe you should have a rough idea before you call.

Our actual quote comes after we've looked at your system. No fees for estimates on new installs. For repairs, we charge a diagnostic fee that gets credited toward the work if you hire us.

Emergency service

Regular hours are Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM. After hours we're still reachable for HVAC emergencies (no heat in winter, no cooling in summer, gas leak concerns). Call the same number, (419) 777-6061. If we can't pick up, leave a message and we call back as fast as we can.

Brands we work with

We install Trane, Air Quest, Rheem, Mitsubishi, and Bradford White as our primary lines. We can service almost any brand; if you have a system we've never seen, we can usually figure it out or tell you when a specialist is the better call.

Service area

Toledo and most of Northwest Ohio. Full list on our service area page. Dedicated info for Perrysburg, Sylvania, Whitehouse, Waterville, and Bowling Green.
