Commercial HVAC

Commercial HVAC for Toledo businesses.

Rooftop units, light commercial installs, preventive maintenance contracts. Offices, restaurants, retail spaces, small warehouses, and rental properties across Northwest Ohio.

What we handle

We work on light commercial HVAC systems across Toledo and the surrounding area:

  • Packaged rooftop units (RTUs) from 3 to 25 tons
  • Split systems for offices and retail spaces
  • Ductless multi-zone systems for mixed-use buildings
  • Make-up air units for restaurants
  • Commercial water heaters
  • Light commercial furnaces and boilers (residential-style equipment in small commercial settings)

We do not handle heavy industrial or chiller work. For chillers, VRF central plants, or anything requiring a boiler operator, we'll refer you to a specialist.

Businesses we serve

  • Small offices (under 10,000 sq ft)
  • Restaurants and bars
  • Retail and boutique storefronts
  • Medical and dental offices
  • Salons and gyms
  • Small warehouses and light manufacturing
  • Rental property management (multi-unit residential, mixed-use)
  • Churches and community buildings

Commercial pricing

Commercial work varies a lot with equipment age and business hours. Rough ranges below. We quote after a site visit for anything custom.

  • Annual PM contract (2 visits, small office)$800 – $1,800

  • 5-ton RTU replacement$12,500 – $18,000

Commercial equipment pricing is heavily affected by crane access, after-hours work requirements, and electrical upgrades. All commercial installs quoted after a site visit.

After-hours service

Business continuity is usually worth more than sleep. We do after-hours and weekend service for commercial clients. Contract customers get priority and flat emergency rates. Non-contract customers pay time-and-a-half after hours.

Preventive maintenance contracts

Most commercial HVAC lives or dies on preventive maintenance. RTUs that get quarterly checks last 15 to 20 years. RTUs that get ignored often need replacement at 10 to 12. We offer contracts at semi-annual, quarterly, and monthly visit frequencies depending on equipment count and usage.

Frequently asked

Do you service rooftop units?

Yes. RTU service, repair, and replacement from 3 to 25 tons is our bread and butter for small commercial. Larger units (over 25 tons) and anything with a chiller plant is outside our scope.

Do you offer preventive maintenance contracts?

Yes. Most small commercial clients do semi-annual (spring + fall) contracts. Restaurants and higher-usage spaces usually go quarterly. Contract pricing depends on equipment count. Ballpark: $800 to $1,800 per year for a single RTU at semi-annual frequency.

Can you handle 24/7 emergency commercial?

Yes for contract customers (included in contract rate). Non-contract customers pay our after-hours rate, which is time and a half. We'll always pick up the phone for a business HVAC emergency.

Experience with restaurants and offices?

Both. Restaurant work is more technical due to make-up air, grease-laden exhaust, and high kitchen loads. Office work is more volume-driven. We handle both but price restaurant work with a slight premium to cover the specialty knowledge.

Do you work on churches and schools?

Churches yes (we've done several). Schools sometimes, depending on the equipment. Larger public school systems typically have dedicated in-house techs or a larger commercial firm on contract, and that's usually the right call for them.
