Working in Bowling Green

BG splits into two worlds for HVAC: established residential neighborhoods (1960s through 1990s single-family homes, mostly standard forced-air systems) and BGSU rental housing (apartments, duplexes, older homes converted to student rentals).

Rental HVAC is its own category. Landlords prioritize reliability and quick fixes over premium equipment. Tenants usually just want heat in winter and cooling in summer without a runaround. We handle both, with flat-rate repair pricing for rental portfolios and same-day response on tenant complaints when possible.

Established BG neighborhoods are similar to Sylvania or Toledo in equipment mix. Most systems are second-generation by now, meaning they're heading toward replacement age. We do a lot of furnace quotes in the fall and AC quotes in late spring.

30 minutes from our shop, down I-75. Response time depends on traffic in the Perrysburg stretch.
