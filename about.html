About us

About Maverick & Miller HVAC | Oregon, OH

Two guys, three decades, one simple idea. Shane Miller and Kindle VanWormer worked for the big HVAC shops around Toledo for years before getting tired of the markups and pressure to upsell. So we started our own.

Why we started this

After fifteen years each in the trade, we kept watching the same thing happen. A homeowner calls with a small problem. A tech gets dispatched, runs the diagnostic, and gets pressured by the shop to quote a full replacement when a $200 repair would have solved it. Commissions, quotas, upgrade incentives. The whole system pushes techs to sell work that doesn't need to happen.

We got tired of it. So in 2024 we left and started Maverick & Miller. No showroom. No sales team. No call center. Just two trained HVAC guys and the equipment we need to do the work right.

Running lean means lower overhead, which means we can charge fair prices on the same premium equipment (Trane, Air Quest, Rheem) that the big shops sell. Same gear, fairer price.

What we actually do

Residential and commercial HVAC across Toledo and Northwest Ohio. Full service list on our services page, but in short:

  • AC repair and new installs
  • Furnace repair and new installs
  • Water heater replacement
  • Ductless mini-split systems
  • Whole-home air filtration
  • Seasonal maintenance and tune-ups
  • Commercial rooftop units and light commercial systems

Where we work

We cover Toledo and the rest of Northwest Ohio, with dedicated focus on Perrysburg, Sylvania, Whitehouse, Waterville, and Bowling Green. Full list of cities we serve on the service area page.

Licensed, insured, and local

We hold an Ohio HVAC contractor license and carry full liability insurance. Both founders are EPA 608 certified. We pull permits when the job requires them (yes, Toledo does require mechanical permits for most water heater and furnace installs).
