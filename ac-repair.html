When to call us

The common signs your AC needs attention:

  • Running but not cooling the house below 76 on a hot day
  • Short cycling (turning on and off every few minutes)
  • Loud screeching, grinding, or banging from the outdoor unit
  • Ice on the refrigerant lines or the indoor coil
  • Water pooling around the indoor unit
  • Circuit breaker tripping when the AC kicks on
  • Energy bills significantly higher than last summer

Any of those, give us a call. We charge an $89 diagnostic fee that covers finding the problem, credited back toward the repair if you hire us.

Repair or replace?

We don't push new installs when a repair is the right call. A modern AC lasts 15 to 20 years. If yours is under 10 and the repair is under $800, we almost always recommend repair. Over that threshold, we'll walk you through the numbers and let you decide.

Signs it's probably replacement time:

  • Unit is 15+ years old and the compressor has failed
  • It uses R-22 refrigerant (banned in 2020, gets expensive to recharge)
  • You've had multiple refrigerant leaks
  • Energy bills have been climbing for 2+ seasons
  • You're considering selling the house soon and want a fresh system as a selling point

Frequently asked

Why is my AC not blowing cold air?

Most common causes in Toledo homes: low refrigerant from a slow leak, a frozen evaporator coil (usually from a clogged filter or low airflow), a failed capacitor on the outdoor unit, or a dirty condenser coil. A diagnostic call usually narrows it down in under 30 minutes.

How much does AC repair cost in Toledo?

Most repairs run between $150 and $800 depending on the part. Capacitor replacement is on the low end. Refrigerant leak repairs and compressor issues are on the high end. A full diagnostic is $89, credited toward the repair if you hire us.

Is it worth repairing a 10-year-old AC?

Usually yes, unless the compressor has failed or you've already had multiple refrigerant leaks. Modern AC units are rated for 15 to 20 years. A 10-year-old unit with a $400 repair typically has plenty of life left. We'll tell you straight if replacement makes more sense.

How fast can you come out for AC repair?

Same-day is typical during AC season (May through September). Call first thing in the morning for best odds of a same-day slot. We reserve emergency spots for no-cool situations when it's over 85 outside.

Do I need a permit for a new AC install?

Toledo requires mechanical permits for new AC installations. We pull the permit as part of the job and handle the inspection. The permit fee is rolled into our quote, no surprise line items.
